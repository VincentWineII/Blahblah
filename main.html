About Us

We believe, that every single person has been through a hard time at one point or another in their life, and always found it very hard to talk to other's about whatever it is they are going through. From homelessness, break-ups, where you just have no direction or will, all the way to serious thoughts about harming yourself or others. We are here for you. We want to help you find the resources you deserve and connect you to people who actually care. Instead of bottling it up inside or complaints falling on deaf ears.
By signing up and making a profile with us, we ensure that all your personal information is kept confidental, while still allowing you to connect with other's from all around the world, who, either have the need to connect with someone for support that has no other way of doing so, or maybe someone who has been in your exact position before, and just may hold the key that will give you the ability to get through this. Never do it on your own. People support people. Especially in a time of need. Time and time again, we have seen how people have stepped up and come together for others in major points of disasters and heartache. Whatever reason you have to hold all of this bottled up inside, now you have no excuse any longer. We will never judge you. Here at Blahblah, we take pride in making sure that you have all the freedom to talk about whatever it is that is bothering you whenever you need to.

Blahblah, Go talk about it!
